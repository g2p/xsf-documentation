Jabber :: People :: Chris Mullins

Chris Mullins, a member of the XMPP Council for 2006-2007, is the Chief Software Architect for JSF Sponsor Coversant. In that role, Chris oversees the architecture for the SoapBox Server and SoapBox Framework. Other duties include acting as a technology evangelist for open standards and interoperability, evaluating technology based decisions, and even writing a little bit of code from time to time. Chris's weblog can be found at <http://www.coversant.net/blogs/cmullins>.
