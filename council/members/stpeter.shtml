Peter Saint-Andre

Peter Saint-Andre is Executive Director of the Jabber Software Foundation (JSF) and author of the XMPP RFCs. He has served as the XMPP Extensions Editor since the founding of the JSF in 2001 and as Chair of the XMPP Council for 2004-2005, 2005-2006, and 2006-2007. Peter's weblog can be found at <http://www.saint-andre.com/blog/>.
