Ralph Meijer

Ralph Meijer is the lead developer of several Jabber-related projects and services, including the Idavoll pubsub component, the Mimir notification service, and Planet Jabber. Ralph has been a member of the XMPP Council for 2004-2005, 2005-2006, and 2006-2007. His weblog is located at <http://ralphm.net/blog/>.
