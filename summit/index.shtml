XMPP DevCon

The XMPP Standards Foundation holds a developer conference twice a year, usually co-located with FOSDEM in Europe (winter) and OSCON in North America (summer). Information about the following meetings is available:

  • DevCon 4 (Brussels, Belgium, February 2008)
  • DevCon 3 (Portland, Oregon, July 2007)
  • DevCon 2 (Brussels, Belgium, February 2007)
  • DevCon 1 (Portland, Oregon, July 2006)
