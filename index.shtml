XMPP Standards Foundation

This website provides complete information about the Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol (XMPP), an open XML communications technology developed by the Jabber open-source community in 1999, formalized by the IETF in 2002-2004, and continuously extended through the standards process of the XMPP Standards Foundation.
