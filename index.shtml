XMPP.org

This website aims to provide complete information about the Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol (XMPP), an XML communications technology originally developed by the Jabber open-source community and since formalized by the IETF.

Visit the following pages for further information:

  • About XMPP
  • XMPP RFCs
  • XMPP Internet-Drafts
  • XMPP Extensions
  • XMPP Registrar
  • XMPP Council
  • XML Schemas
