XMPP Internet-Drafts

Last Updated: 2006-09-25

The base XMPP specifications are RFC 3920 and RFC 3921. While most XMPP extensions are defined in the XEP series published by the XMPP Technology Forum, the following Internet-Drafts are under consideration within the IETF:

  • draft-ietf-sieve-notify-xmpp-02 (text | HTML) -- XMPP notifications for the Sieve email filtering language

  • draft-saintandre-atompub-notify-05 (text | HTML) -- transporting Atom syndication data over the XMPP pubsub extension

  • draft-saintandre-jabberid-04 (text | HTML) -- including XMPP addresses in email headers

  • draft-saintandre-xmpp-simple-08 (text | HTML) -- enabling basic interoperability between XMPP and SIMPLE

Older versions of these and other drafts are available in the attic (see also the CVS repository).
