Public XMPP Services

There are tens of thousands of XMPP services deployed on the Internet. In addition to large, well-known services such as Google Talk, Live Journal Talk, Nimbuzz, and Ovi, there are also many smaller services run by volunteers in the XMPP community. The following table lists the public XMPP services that have registered with the XSF to be listed here, but there are many more such services on the Internet so this is not a complete list!
