XSF Contact Page

Before sending a question to the XMPP Standards Foundation, check out the jabber.org status page, Jabber User Guide, general FAQ, end-user FAQ, developer FAQ, jabberd admin guide, and other documentation.

Also, the XMPP Standards Foundation provides public mailing lists related to Jabber/XMPP programming, server administration, client use, and protocol development. Please post there first, since you will probably receive a faster response!

If your question is not answered in available documentation and is not appropriate for one of the public mailing lists, feel free to contact XSF Executive Director Peter Saint-Andre with any questions or concerns.

You may also contact the XMPP Standards Foundation at the following postal address:

XMPP Standards Foundation
P.O. Box 1641
Denver, CO 80201-1641
USA

Financial contributions are accepted (mostly from XSF Sponsors) either by check to the foregoing mailing address or by PayPal to <paypal@jabber.org>. Please note that at this time contributions to the XMPP Standards Foundation are not tax-deductible.
