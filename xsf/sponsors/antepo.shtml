XSF Sponsors :: Antepo

Note: In early 2007, Antepo was acquired by Adobe Systems.

Antepo, Inc., a Silver Sponsor of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), is the developer of Open Presence Network (OPN), a standards-based, carrier-class, enterprise IM system. Antepo OPN Systems allow companies to employ real-time communications to expedite mission-critical business processes. OPN Systems connect users across protocols and enterprise boundaries, linking fixed and mobile Instant Messaging environments into interoperable, extended collaborative networks. Antepo provides software and related professional services to telecommunication companies, financial institutions and globally distributed companies in the US and internationally. The company is based in New York, with offices internationally.

Antepo offered the first commercial Java-based XMPP server, delivering standard integration with Java based networks and services. Its systems deliver multi-processor scalability and reliability through load sharing hardware configurations. Versions are available for Windows, UNIX and Linux platforms. Antepo additionally offers clients for desktop, PocketPC, mobile phones and other handheld devices.

Antepo has been a technical participant in the XSF, as well as a leader in forums such as the Java JAIN expert group on Presence since early 2003.

Evaluation servers, clients, and Java SDKs can be obtained from <www.antepo.com>.
