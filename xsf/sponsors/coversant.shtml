XSF Sponsors :: Coversant

Coversant™, a Silver Sponsor of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), is a leading communications software provider that leverages new standards to meet the emerging demand for worldwide electronic communications.

Coversant provides XMPP-based, ultra-secure, federated communication products and services for customers who demand outstanding reliability, scalability, security, interoperability, and extensibility. All products are compliant with the XMPP global standard for messaging and presence.

Coversant provides solutions for a variety of collaboration environments including the Windows desktop, Linux desktop, mobile, and web environments. Products in the SoapBox Collaboration Platform, including SoapBox Server, SoapBox Framework, and SoapBox Client, form an ecosystem of integrated collaboration solutions.

For more information about Coversant, visit <www.coversant.net>.

(Note: Prior to 2005, Coversant sponsored the XSF under its former business name, Winfessor.)
