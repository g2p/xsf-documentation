XSF Sponsors :: USSHC

The US Secure Hosting Center (USSHC), a Gold Sponsor of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), is a privately-owned, underground colocation data center located in the rural Midwestern United States. USSHC offers the most secure, carrier-class, disaster-tolerant colocation data center services available. Since the founding of the XSF in 2001, USSHC has hosted the XSF's server infrastructure, including the web, mail, and source control servers for xmpp.org and the production XMPP server at jabber.org.

For more information about USSHC, visit <www.usshc.com>.
