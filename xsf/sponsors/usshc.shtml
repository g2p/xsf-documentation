XSF Sponsors :: USSHC

The US Secure Hosting Center (USSHC) is a privately-owned, underground colocation data center located in the rural Midwestern United States. USSHC offers the most secure, carrier-class, disaster-resistant colocation data center services available. Since the founding of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF) in 2001, USSHC has hosted the XSF's server infrastructure, including the web, mail, and source control servers for xmpp.org and the production XMPP server at jabber.org.

For more information about USSHC, visit <www.usshc.com>.
