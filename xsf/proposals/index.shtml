XSF Proposals

The following proposals have been officially presented to the membership or the Board of the XMPP Standards Foundation (or its predecessor the Jabber Software Foundation):

  1. Protocol Branding (Q2 2006)
  2. Intermediate Certification Authority (Q4 2006)
  3. XMPP Standards Foundation (Q4 2006)
  4. Strengthening Trust in XMPP Technologies (Q1 2007)
  5. Clarifying the Purposes of the XSF (Q1 2007)
