XSF People :: Peter Saint-Andre

Peter Saint-Andre is Director of Standards at Jabber, Inc. He has been contributing to the Jabber/XMPP community since late 1999, focusing on protocol standardization as editor of the XMPP RFCs and author of numerous XMPP extension protocols. In addition, he has served as Executive Director of the XMPP Standards Foundation since 2002 and as Chair of the XMPP Council since 2004.

Peter's weblog is published at stpeter.im and syndicated at Planet Jabber. His contact information is available here.
