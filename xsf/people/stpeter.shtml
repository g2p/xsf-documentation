XSF People :: Peter Saint-Andre

Peter Saint-Andre is Executive Director of the XMPP Standards Foundation, Director of Standards at Jabber Inc., Chair of the XMPP Council, and managing editor of the XSF's standards process. Peter has been contributing to the Jabber/XMPP community since late 1999, focusing on protocol documentation and development as editor of the XMPP RFCs and author of numerous XMPP protocol extensions. His weblog is published at stpeter.im and syndicated at Planet Jabber.
