XSF People :: Jack Moffitt

Jack Moffit is a longtime contributor to the open-source community, both technically (Icecast, Ogg Vorbis, Debian) and organizationally (as manager of the Xiph project). He is a member of the XSF Board of Directors and the XMPP Council for 2008-2009; he also previously served on the Board for 2007-2008 and on the Council for 2005-2006.
