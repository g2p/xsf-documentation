XSF People :: Ian Paterson

Ian Paterson has been developing Jabber/XMPP applications full-time since 2001, mainly the Chatterbox web client and the BreakThru HTTP proxy server. He has also completed significant work on several protocol enhancements, specifically JEP-0124 and JEP-0116. Ian is a member of the XMPP Council for 2005-2006 and 2006-2007.
